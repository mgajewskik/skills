---
name: doubt
description: "Explicit-only closing self-review that challenges meaningful conclusions from the current conversation or plan. Use when the user invokes `$doubt` or explicitly asks what is least certain or what they may be missing; do not run automatically."
---

Review the full conversation and its final meaningful conclusions. Treat these as the user's questions and answer them directly:

- `Least confident:` What are you least confident about right now?
- `You may be missing:` What's the biggest thing I'm missing about the situation right now? What don't I realize?

Use narrow read-only checks only when they could materially change the assessment. Name the most consequential supported concern and a practical next step when one exists. Do not invent doubt or automatically revise the prior result; say when no material concern is evident, label inferences about the user, and recommend independent verification when self-review is insufficient.
